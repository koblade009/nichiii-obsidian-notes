Subcategory of [[Catalyst]]

These use [[Standardized Sapphire]] as source of [[Mana]] for casting [[Spell]]s.
When the [[Sapphire Slot]] is depleted, the [[Standardized Sapphire]] crumbles into dust. Which can then be replaced.

Construction:
The [[Sapphire Slot]] is connected to the handle with [[Silver]], which is a [[Mana]] resonant metal
