Blade

Also known as the shredder

Guild leader of [[Bloody Cross]]

Has a controversial public image. 
Scorned by the public but beloved by his guild members.

Established [[Bloody Cross]] as a way to provide jobs to his friends. Eventually, all of his friends died and he kept the guild running to provide jobs to young hardworking people.


Eventually had to disband [[Bloody Cross]] due to locals complaining about the guild's existence. Slander about how the guild provides assassination services as well as accussations of murdering rival guild members to earn more jobs. When in fact, the guild only accepted dungeon delving/monster hunting quests and has manage to siphon most quests simply due to Jeff's connections

Family friend of [[Bonnie Davidsen Merriweather]]
He is a close friend of Bonnie's father. Bonnie was sent to join [[Bloody Cross]] by her parents as a way to train her.

Retired entirely from the adventurer life. Now lives a quiet life with his family. Owns a bakery.
