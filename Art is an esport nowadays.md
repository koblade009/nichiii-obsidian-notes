
I used to think of art as self expression but there are negatives to it:
- isolation
- lack of connection with audience
- puts too much emphasis on myself, which means I'm often unaware of how other artists go about developing their art

Then I started to think of it as manufacturing / design, but that still doesn't work:
- it puts too much emphasis on selling, which forces stronger connection with audience
- however it disconnects the artist from their art

I think the healthiest way to view it now is to look at it as if it's an esport like dota:
- i think it's apt, you have to put in the time to develop your own strats
- but at the same time, you're also looking at other artists and potentially get something from their strats
- most importantly, it's skill expression. youre expressing yourself but at the same time it's not just about you
- there is an audience too, there are people who believe in you and you should appreciate and try not to let down
