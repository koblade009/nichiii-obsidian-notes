Oddball swordsman


Former apprentice of [[Rhea - Witch of War]]
Close friend of [[Mob]] and [[Kraul]] - The three are childhood friends

Now works as a freelance mercenary for hire. Hangs out in taverns. Advertises his services on the bulletin board of the town he's in.

Specializes in human vs human combat.

He is a coward in a sense that he will do any despicable act in order to survive
In spite of that, he is still a very skilled swordsman. He's just unconventional in the way that he sets up his fights, always making sure to put his opponents at a disadvantaged situation before engaging with them directly.

Rhea sees him as her son
He doesn't reciprocate this however and only looks at her as his master
He is aware that his parents are still alive. And he is currently searching for them.

He got this information from his brother Ken (close friend of [[John Merriweather Goldman]])
