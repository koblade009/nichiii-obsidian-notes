# Takeaways
- Don't focus too much on technical mastery because advancements in technology will eventually lessen its value. 
	- Example: Digital art, around early 2000s the skill ceiling was not quite as high, due to Photoshop being the only real program artists can use. Then clip studio paint arrived and with it came 3d models to trace over. Suddenly people started using it and now, perfect perspective is the norm. Then digital art courses became more prominent, pushing the standard for rendering (for hired work) even higher. And now, AI image gens are becoming more and more accessible and better.
